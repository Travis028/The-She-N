My Beloved Nidah
, Every time I sit down to write about you, I find myself lost in thoughts of everything you mean to me. How do I put into words a feeling so deep, so powerful, that it takes over my whole heart and soul? Nidah, loving you is the most beautiful thing that has ever happened in my life, and I want you to know just how much you mean to me. From the moment you became a part of my life, everything changed. The world became brighter, my days became sweeter, and my nights became warmer because I know that somewhere out there, I have someone as precious as you thinking of me too. You are the reason I smile for no reason, the reason my heart beats faster, the reason I dream bigger. You’ve turned my life into something magical, and I never want this feeling to end. I love the way your name sounds—it’s like music to my heart. I love the way your presence calms me, the way your words bring peace to my mind, and the way your love gives meaning to everything I do. Nidah, you are not just someone I care about; you are the center of my world, the queen of my heart, and the most beautiful part of my life. When I think about the future, I see you in every dream I have. I see us walking together through life’s ups and downs, holding each other close, building a love that grows stronger every single day. I don’t just want you in my life—I need you. You are my sunshine on the darkest days, my strength when I feel weak, my everything when I feel like I have nothing. If I could take all the stars from the sky and give them to you, I would—because even the brightest star cannot outshine the light you bring into my life. If I could write your name on every piece of sand on every beach, I would—because I want the world to know that my heart belongs to you and only you. Nidah, I want you to know this: I am yours. Every heartbeat, every breath, every thought—I give it all to you. You are my one, my only, my forever. No matter where life takes us, I promise you this—I will love you today, tomorrow, and every single day after that, for the rest of my life. Thank you for being you—so beautiful, so loving, so amazing. You are the best thing that has ever happened to me, and I will never stop loving you. My heart, my soul, my everything—it’s all yours, Nidah. Forever and always, Wayne
